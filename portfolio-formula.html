Project Description

As a machinist for the University of Toronto Formula Racing Team (UTFR), I got first-hand experience with the car by machining spare parts for competition. This included reading engineering drawings and realizing them with the lathe, mill, and drill-press as long as precise measurement instruments such as the micrometer and calipers.
